## Score Band: Weak Go

## Recommendation

Weak Go -- solid secondary market. API is ready, data is critical, gap is real with limited competition. But MSP channel is underdeveloped and total addressable market is smaller than M365. Good for a vendor already in enterprise content backup, not a primary TAM driver alone.

## Key Risks

- Box reps tell users "Box is already your backup"
- MSP channel weak
- Market traction nascent (HYCU only entered Nov 2024)
