## Score Band: Strong Go

## Recommendation

Strong Go -- maximum scores across nearly all dimensions. Largest addressable market in SaaS backup. Competition is real (20-30 vendors) but penetration is only ~6%. New Backup Storage API creates differentiation opportunity.

## Key Risks

- Intense competition from well-funded incumbents (Veeam, Acronis, Druva)
- Teams chat backup complexity
- Commodity pricing pressure ($3/user/mo benchmark)
