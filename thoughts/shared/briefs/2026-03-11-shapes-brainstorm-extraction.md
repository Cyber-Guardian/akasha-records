# Shapes Brainstorm Board — Full Idea Extraction

All ideas extracted from the brainstorming canvas, organized by theme.

## 1. Core Values & Philosophy

- **Trustworthy** — the #1 brand attribute
- **Reliable**
- **Powerfully Complex (but not complicated)**
- Convey Trust = speed/responsiveness = quality
- "How else can we convey Trust?"

### UX Philosophy
- **Highly Configurable** but "Complex, not complicated"
- **Sane defaults** — stay approachable (simple enough) for new IT, but offer high configurability for those who need/want it
- **Executive Summary** views for decision-makers

## 2. Core Backup & Recovery Features

| Idea | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Inplace recovery** | Intersection of "Industry IT Needs" and "General IT Needs" — the core value prop |

## 7. Market Strategy

### Target Industries (pipeline)
Legal, Education, Financial, Healthcare, Government, Insurance, Pharma, Heavy Industries/Manufacturing, Administrative and Support Services

**Strategy:** "We adopt new industries by first adopting clouds used by them, later add specialized features"

**Question:** "Which industries have specific clouds to backup or more general like O365?"

### Target Segments
- SMB/Mid Market MSPs
- Enterprise MSPs
- SMB Lawfirms (likely no internal IT)
- Mid Market/Enterprise Lawfirms
- K-12 School Districts
- Colleges
- Banks?

### Go-to-Market Checklist
- Is the target customer well-funded? **Yes**
- Is the target customer readily accessible to your sales force?
- Does the target customer have a compelling reason to buy? **Yes**
- Can we today, deliver a whole product? **Yes**
- Is there entrenched competition that could block us?
- If we win this segment, can we leverage it to enter additional segments?
- Is the market consistent with the values, passions, and goals of the founding team?
- How quickly can we win this market?

### Persona Note
- Think of different IT end users, economic buyers, and champions
- Lawyers hate user-based pricing, like usage-based pricing

## 10. Cloud Viability Ranking Framework

**Question:** "How would we rank the viability of a cloud to partner with?"

### Market Upside
- ARR
- How integral they are to the firms' operations/workflows
- Has partnership programs/marketplace
- Has preexisting competition

### Technical Feasibility
- API limitations are reasonable or extendable
- Has encompassing API
- Data cardinality vs Data size
- Restore complexity
- TOS allows backups
- OAuth flow exists

### Demand Signal
- "Backup for X" search volume
