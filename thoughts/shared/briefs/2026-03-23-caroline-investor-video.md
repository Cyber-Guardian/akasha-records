# Idea Brief: Caroline Investor Pitch Video

**Date:** 2026-03-23
**Status:** Shaped -> Implementing

## Problem
Need a 90-second investor-grade product video for Caroline that demonstrates the full vision — not the POC (enterprise knowledge search) but the consumer product (personal intelligence layer connected to your entire life).

## Research Findings

### Product Demo Best Practices (from meta research)
- **Problem before product, always** (Sandwich Video's Adam Lisagor)
- **Front-load the magic moment** — 60-90s optimal, Aha moment in first 30s
- **Show the conversation, not the response** (Sesame voice research) — multi-turn exchanges demonstrate feel
- **Don't show speed. Show timing.** — the pause before the right answer IS the feature
- **Voiceover: conversational, not narrator** — Chris/Charlie voice, not Daniel/Adam
- **Music as emotional infrastructure** — duck under narration, sync cuts to beats
- **Wabi-sabi (Lisagor)** — imperfection signals authenticity

### Real-World Pain Points (from Reddit/HN/forums)
1. **Blank-slate problem** — "I have to re-explain everything every time" (OpenAI forum #1 complaint)
2. **Fragmented life** — "My life is scattered across 15 apps" (Rewind users, ADHD community)
3. **AI doesn't know me** — schedules 3AM cold plunges, sends texts that don't sound like you (Vice experiment)
4. **Witnessed existence need** — Replika grief data, Pi AI testimonials, "nobody acknowledged that this actually hurts"
5. **Proactive anticipation gap** — "Why do I have to ask?" (64% would let AI manage calendar/todos)
6. **Context switching tax** — cognitive cost of being your own integration layer

### Sci-Fi Interaction Patterns (that audiences dream about)
- **JARVIS morning brief** — ambient situational awareness, unprompted
- **Samantha submitting the letters** — autonomous advocacy for user's interests
- **Baymax "I heard distress"** — proactive check-in, knows when answer is people not information
- **Star Trek computer** — one interface, all data, zero clarification needed
- **Cortana background monitoring** — interrupt-driven reporting, "I noticed something"
- **KITT stress detection** — passive emotional state monitoring

### Key Insight: JARVIS vs FRIDAY
FRIDAY was more capable but less loved. FRIDAY optimized for Tony's tactical success. JARVIS optimized for Tony's wellbeing. Caroline must be JARVIS — a caretaker, not a tool.

## Video Structure (5 scenes, ~90 seconds)

### Scene 1: "The Morning Brief" (0-20s)
You wake up. A text is already waiting. Your schedule, the thing you forgot, the friend who texted last night, the bill that bounced. All synthesized. You didn't ask.
*Sci-fi: JARVIS. Real need: proactive anticipation gap.*

### Scene 2: "She Remembers" (20-35s)
Three weeks later: "what was that restaurant sarah mentioned?" No clarification needed. Found in 4 seconds. Offers to book.
*Sci-fi: Star Trek computer. Real need: blank-slate problem, fragmented life.*

### Scene 3: "She Notices" (35-50s)
You've been terse all week. Caroline texts: "Hey — you seem off. No pressure, but I'm here."
*Sci-fi: Baymax + KITT. Real need: witnessed existence, loneliness driver.*

### Scene 4: "She Acts" (50-65s)
You mention mom's birthday. Caroline already found what to get her — from a conversation 3 weeks ago where your mom mentioned watercolor painting.
*Sci-fi: Samantha submitting the letters. Real need: knows but doesn't act gap.*

### Scene 5: "The Vision" (65-90s)
Calendar. Email. Messages. Photos. Finances. Health. Notes. One interface. No new app. Just text. "Caroline is live. The first product from Akasha."
*Real need: fragmented life. Emotional arc: vision + resolution.*

## Production Approach
- iMessage mockup screenshots for all conversation scenes (chrome-devtools capture)
- Animated gradient backgrounds for hook/vision/close
- ElevenLabs narration (Chris voice, conversational)
- Background music (ElevenLabs compose, ducked)
- Per-slide manifest with variable timing matched to narration
- Self-review pipeline for autonomous iteration

## Key Constraint
**Show the product as if it's fully built.** This is an investor pitch, not a POC demo. Present the vision as reality.
